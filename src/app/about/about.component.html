{{"Our Story"|translate}}

UFeed was born from the heart of the Arab African world, with a mission to transform the Human Capital domain. We are unique in that we integrate technology while leveraging 30 years of mastery through to ignite tech-savvy change.

UFeed is the spark that turns decades of consulting wisdom into accessible, AI-powered tools, crafted by regional experts for a global stage. Our platform is fully enabled in both Arabic and English, ensuring that we cater to the diverse needs of our users.

Our journey is fueled by a powerful purpose: to break down the barriers of traditional consulting and democratize human capital excellence. We empower every organization—from startups to giant corporation—with tools once reserved for the elite, as well enabling consulting businesses to address their clients' needs seamlessly and efficiently by adopting our platform. We focus on building internal capacity for our clients, tapping into the untapped knowledge base vertical in Human Capital to support data-driven decision-making.

With UFeed, organizations can establish industry benchmarks and norms, guided by well-defined tools and data analytics that ensure success and cost efficiency for both our clients and their customers. Coming from the region, we understand how to serve our local market using global expertise infused with local flavor. UFeed is committed to harnessing cutting-edge technology, paving the way toward a fully AI/ML-powered model that enhances the future of human capital management.
